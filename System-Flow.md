SYSTEM FLOW

End-to-End Intelligence Workflow

The purpose of the system flow is to describe how information moves through COGNRXA AI™.

STEP 1

User uploads information.

Examples:

• Reports

• Research Papers

• Historical Records

• Documents

↓

STEP 2

Knowledge Extraction Engine processes information.

Output:

• Concepts

• Topics

• Entities

• Relationships

↓

STEP 3

Pattern Discovery Engine analyzes extracted knowledge.

Output:

• Trends

• Repeated Events

• Similar Cases

• Emerging Signals

↓

STEP 4

Retrospective Analysis Engine evaluates historical context.

Output:

• Lessons Learned

• Historical Understanding

• Experience-Based Insights

↓

STEP 5

Insight Generation Engine creates summaries.

Output:

• Findings

• Observations

• Knowledge Reports

↓

STEP 6

Decision Support Engine provides contextual assistance.

Output:

• Recommendations

• Historical References

• Evidence-Based Understanding

↓

STEP 7

Dashboard displays intelligence.

Final Outputs:

• Insight Reports

• Pattern Reports

• Decision Support Summaries

• Knowledge Maps

• Learning Reports

This workflow represents the MVP intelligence cycle.
