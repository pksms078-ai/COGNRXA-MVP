CONTRIBUTING TO COGNRXA AI™

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

COGNRXA AI™ is a research-driven initiative focused on transforming historical information into actionable intelligence.

Contributors may participate in:

- Research
- Documentation
- Knowledge Modeling
- Data Engineering
- Software Development
- User Experience Design
- Testing
- Pilot Programs

---

Contribution Process

Step 1

Review project documentation.

Step 2

Identify an issue or improvement.

Step 3

Create a proposal.

Step 4

Submit for review.

Step 5

Receive feedback.

Step 6

Implement approved changes.

---

Code Standards

- Clear naming conventions
- Modular architecture
- Documented functions
- Version control compliance

---

Research Standards

- Evidence-based reasoning
- Transparent assumptions
- Reproducible methodology
- Ethical considerations

---

Core Principle

Technology should augment human intelligence rather than replace human judgment.
