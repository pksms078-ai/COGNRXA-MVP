System Architecture Overview

COGNRXA AI™ follows a modular architecture.

The MVP architecture consists of six major layers.

Layer 1 — Information Input

Sources:

• Documents

• Reports

• Research Papers

• Historical Records

• Knowledge Archives

• Structured Datasets

↓

Layer 2 — Knowledge Extraction Engine

Functions:

• Entity Extraction

• Topic Identification

• Relationship Detection

• Metadata Generation

↓

Layer 3 — Pattern Discovery Engine

Functions:

• Trend Analysis

• Similarity Detection

• Relationship Mapping

• Pattern Recognition

↓

Layer 4 — Retrospective Analysis Engine

Functions:

• Historical Analysis

• Lesson Extraction

• Experience Mapping

• Context Generation

↓

Layer 5 — Insight Generation Engine

Functions:

• Observation Creation

• Insight Summarization

• Knowledge Synthesis

• Contextual Interpretation

↓

Layer 6 — Decision Support Engine

Functions:

• Recommendation Support

• Historical References

• Evidence Mapping

• Understanding Enhancement

↓

User Dashboard

Outputs:

• Knowledge Maps

• Pattern Reports

• Historical Insights

• Decision Support Reports

• Interactive Intelligence Dashboard

The modular architecture allows future expansion without redesigning the entire system.
